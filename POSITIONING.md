# Sipiary — Positioning

**One-liner:** Every other wine app is a store or a spreadsheet, and every social
network is an attention machine. Sipiary is the only place that treats wine as a
personal journey worth savoring — beginner to expert — with a biodynamic soul
nobody else has.

> Honest framing: large platforms still win on scale and network effects.
> Sipiary wins on focus and experience *for wine lovers*.

## vs. mainstream social media (Instagram / TikTok / X)

| Sipiary | The big platforms |
|---|---|
| No ads, no doomscroll — built to enjoy wine, not maximize screen time | Engagement-optimized, ad-driven |
| Privacy-first — logs (and profiles) can be private; no ad-targeting | Your data is the product |
| No vanity-metric pressure — self-likes blocked; it's your taste journey | Like/follower counts drive anxiety |
| Substance — structured tasting notes, real palate data | Optimized for virality |
| Age-gated & legally compliant for alcohol from day one | Generic |

## vs. wine apps (Vivino / Untappd / CellarTracker / Delectable)

| Feature | Why Sipiary wins |
|---|---|
| 🌙 Biodynamic Lunar Calendar | Nobody else has it — real moon phases + fruit/root/flower/leaf days |
| Range: beginner → expert | 2-tap mood log *and* WSET-style tasting sheets |
| 🧬 Taste Match % | Palate-compatibility score between users — a social hook no wine app has |
| Vibe Deck | Tinder-style mood discovery, zero jargon |
| "Your Month in Wine" recap | Spotify-Wrapped for wine — shareable, free |
| Persona onboarding | Adapts to skill level instead of one-size-fits-all |
| Shareable wine cards + QR | Instagram-ready cards that double as free marketing |
| Enjoyment-first, not a store | Competitors push you to buy; Sipiary keeps the UX free and clean |
| Pour rating | Tactile, on-brand rating instead of generic stars |

## Under-the-hood advantages
- Fast on mobile — photos auto-shrink ~96%, cached a year, lazy-loaded
- Installable PWA — works like a native app, no app store
- Real-time notifications via WebSocket
- No iPhone zoom-jank when typing

## The niche to own
The biodynamic / natural-wine angle is the defensible differentiator. Lean into
it (the Lunar Calendar in the daily loop, natural-wine discovery) rather than
competing head-on with Vivino on catalogue size or Instagram on scale.
